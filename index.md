## Introduction

QR Studio gives you everything you need to scan and generate QR codes and barcodes on your Android or iOS device. Whether you're a shopper comparing prices, a developer sharing a link, or a small business owner creating labels, QR Studio is built to be fast, accurate, and beautifully simple.

Unlike many other scanner apps, **QR Studio does not require an internet connection, does not collect personal data, and does not show ads**. You scan, you generate, you share — that's it.

---

## Key Features at a Glance

- 🔎 **QR Code Scanner** — Instant, accurate scanning with your device camera.
- 🧩 **QR Code Generator** — Create QR codes for URLs, text, emails, phone numbers, contacts, Wi-Fi, and more.
- 📦 **Barcode Generator** — Generate barcodes in popular formats such as EAN-13, UPC-A, Code 128, and QR.
- 🕓 **Scan History** — Access your previously scanned codes anytime, stored locally on your device.
- 📤 **Save & Share** — Save generated codes as images or share them through any app on your phone.
- ⚡ **Fast & Lightweight** — Optimized for speed and low battery consumption.
- 📴 **Offline Functionality** — Core features work without an internet connection.
- 🎨 **User-Friendly Interface** — Clean, modern design that anyone can use.
