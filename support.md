## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is QR Studio free to use?**
A: Yes. QR Studio is free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or hidden fees.

**Q: Does QR Studio require an internet connection?**
A: No. All core features — scanning and generating codes — work fully offline.

**Q: Does QR Studio show ads?**
A: No. The app is completely ad-free.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No. QR Studio does not require any account or sign-up.

### Scanning

**Q: Why isn't the scanner reading my code?**
A: Try the following:
- Hold the device steady, 6–12 inches (15–30 cm) away from the code.
- Make sure the entire code is visible inside the viewfinder.
- Ensure adequate lighting; avoid harsh glare.
- Clean your camera lens.

**Q: Can QR Studio scan barcodes as well as QR codes?**
A: Yes. QR Studio supports both QR codes and a wide range of 1D and 2D barcodes.

**Q: Can I scan multiple codes in a row?**
A: Yes. After a successful scan, you can immediately point the camera at the next code.

### Generating

**Q: What types of QR codes can I generate?**
A: QR Studio supports URL, text, email, phone, SMS, contact (vCard), Wi-Fi, and location codes.

**Q: Can I add a logo to my generated QR code?**
A: This feature is on our roadmap. Stay tuned!

**Q: What format is the generated image saved in?**
A: Generated codes are saved as PNG images.

### Privacy & Data

**Q: Where is my scan history stored?**
A: Locally on your device. We do not upload or sync your scan history to any server.

**Q: How do I clear my scan history?**
A: Open the app, go to **Settings → History → Clear History**. You can also delete individual entries by swiping them.

**Q: Does the app access my camera in the background?**
A: No. The camera is only accessed while the scanner screen is open and visible.

For more, see our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.html).

## Troubleshooting

### The app crashes on launch
- Make sure your device is running a supported version of Android or iOS.
- Restart your device.
- Reinstall the app.
- If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and OS version.

### Scans are slow or inaccurate
- Ensure good lighting.
- Clean your camera lens.
- Hold the device steady.
- Update to the latest version of the app.

### The camera shows a black screen
- Close all other apps that may be using the camera.
- Restart the app.
- Check your device's privacy settings to ensure QR Studio has permission to use the camera.
