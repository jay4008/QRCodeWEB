# Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is QR Studio free to use?**
A: Yes. QR Studio is free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or hidden fees.

**Q: Does QR Studio require an internet connection?**
A: No. All core features — scanning and generating codes — work fully offline. You only need an internet connection if you choose to open a scanned URL or share content through an online app.

**Q: Does QR Studio show ads?**
A: No. The app is completely ad-free.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No. QR Studio does not require any account or sign-up.

### Scanning

**Q: Why isn't the scanner reading my code?**
A: Try the following:
- Hold the device steady, 6–12 inches (15–30 cm) away from the code.
- Make sure the entire code is visible inside the viewfinder.
- Ensure adequate lighting; avoid harsh glare.
- Clean your camera lens.
- If the code is on a curved or reflective surface, try flattening it.

**Q: Can QR Studio scan barcodes as well as QR codes?**
A: Yes. QR Studio supports both QR codes and a wide range of 1D and 2D barcodes (EAN, UPC, Code 128, etc.).

**Q: Can I scan multiple codes in a row?**
A: Yes. After a successful scan, you can immediately point the camera at the next code. There's no need to tap a "scan" button.

### Generating

**Q: What types of QR codes can I generate?**
A: QR Studio supports URL, text, email, phone, SMS, contact (vCard), Wi-Fi, and location codes. See the [Features](features.html) page for full details.

**Q: Can I add a logo to my generated QR code?**
A: This feature is on our roadmap. Stay tuned by checking the [Changelog](changelog.html).

**Q: What format is the generated image saved in?**
A: Generated codes are saved as PNG images, suitable for both screen and print.

### Privacy & Data

**Q: Where is my scan history stored?**
A: Locally on your device. We do not upload or sync your scan history to any server.

**Q: How do I clear my scan history?**
A: Open the app, go to **Settings → History → Clear History**. You can also delete individual entries by swiping them in the history list.

**Q: Does the app access my camera in the background?**
A: No. The camera is only accessed while the scanner screen is open and visible.

For more on privacy, see our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.html).

## Troubleshooting

### The app crashes on launch

- Make sure your device is running a supported version of Android or iOS (see the store listing for current minimum versions).
- Restart your device.
- Reinstall the app — your scan history will be cleared, so back it up first if needed.
- If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and OS version.

### Scans are slow or inaccurate

- Ensure you have good lighting.
- Clean your camera lens.
- Hold the device steady.
- Update to the latest version of the app — we regularly improve scanner performance.

### A generated code won't scan on another device

- Verify that the code was generated at a sufficient size and resolution.
- Try increasing the error-correction level (Settings → Generator → Error Correction).
- Make sure the code is not being printed with a low-contrast color combination.

### The camera shows a black screen

- Close all other apps that may be using the camera.
- Restart the app.
- Restart your device.
- Check your device's privacy settings to ensure QR Studio has permission to use the camera.

### I've lost my scan history

- Scan history is stored locally on your device only. We cannot recover it for you.
- To prevent future loss, consider exporting your history (Settings → History → Export) periodically.

### Battery drains quickly while scanning

- This is normal when the camera is in continuous use. The app is optimized for low power, but a constantly active camera will still consume battery.
- Close the scanner screen when not actively scanning.
